During our initial discussion, the car wash owner explained that the business currently suffered from a very low social media presence and barely engaged in active marketing, relying almost entirely on drive-by local traffic and bar customers. 
To solve this, we discussed designing a professional webpage to help market the business online and encapsulate their washing packages in an enticing, visually structured manner that is easy to view. He emphasized that their target audience consisted of busy local professionals and families who valued speed and thoroughness, and he specifically requested that the website's color palette match their signature brand blue seen on their physical banner in the car wash.
Ultimately, the owner expressed that the primary goal of the website should be to clearly display their competitive pricing and contact details, making it as effortless as possible for potential clients to find the shop or call.
